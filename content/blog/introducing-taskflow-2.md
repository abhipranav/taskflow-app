- See overdue tasks that need immediate attention

## Quick Capture Widget

We've also added a **Quick Capture** feature. Press `Q` anywhere to instantly capture a task without losing context. The widget includes:

- Voice-to-task input (speak your task)
- Smart board/column selection
- AI-suggested priority levels
